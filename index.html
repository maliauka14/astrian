FAQ

1. Do you develop apps for both Android and iOS?

Yes, our company specializes in developing high-quality mobile solutions for both Android and iOS platforms. While our roots are in iOS development, we have expanded our expertise to offer fully cross-platform applications using modern technologies. This includes not only mobile app development but also backend architecture, cloud integration, and complete UI/UX design. Whether you need a native app or a shared codebase for faster delivery, we tailor the approach to best fit your business goals.

2. How long does it take to develop an app?

The development timeline depends largely on the app’s size, complexity, and feature set. On average, our projects range from 2 weeks to 3–4 months. For example, a small utility app with minimal features can be completed in as little as 2.5 weeks. However, a more complex business solution — including features like real-time data sync, admin dashboards, user authentication, and backend infrastructure — can take 3 months or longer. We also offer project planning support to help define realistic timelines based on your needs.

3. Can you help with app design as well?

Yes, we offer complete UI/UX design services as part of our development process. Our design team focuses on creating intuitive, visually engaging interfaces that offer a seamless user experience. From wireframes and user flows to interactive prototypes and final designs, we work closely with you to ensure the app looks and feels exactly how your users expect. We also consider platform-specific design guidelines to ensure your app performs and feels native on both Android and iOS devices.

4. Do you offer post-launch support and updates?

Absolutely. We believe that launching an app is just the beginning. We offer ongoing maintenance and support packages that include bug fixes, performance monitoring, feature updates, and compatibility checks with new OS versions. Whether you need regular updates or occasional improvements, our team stays involved to make sure your app continues to deliver value to your users and keeps up with evolving technology and business needs
